I am a PhD student in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at UC San Diego (UCSD) co-advised by Prof. Alex Snoeren and Prof. George Porter. I am affiliated with the Sysnet lab at UCSD where I am working on various aspects of datacenter networking. I am currently playing around with P4 switches among other things. Specifically, I am emulating various network topologies on a single programmable switch. It's fun to see what modern switches are capable of doing!

I am passionate about networked systems and enjoy building and hacking stuff. When I'm not working hard, I'm playing hard: I am a cycling enthusiast, especially mountain biking. I love outdoor activities in general.
